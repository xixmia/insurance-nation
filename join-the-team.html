Who We ARE

Insurance Nation is an innovative and growing insurance company specializing in personal and business insurance policies ranging from Home, Auto, and Commercial Property to Life Insurance and Bonds. Insurance Nation prides itself in its customer service and professionalism.  We have built a proven and successful team of over 85 members serving over 22,000 active policies throughout Florida in the Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach and Monroe Counties.  

WHAT WE OFFER

We provide the opportunity for professional growth and a fulfilling career in the insurance industry.  Best of all, there is no prior experience or background in insurance needed.  Insurance Nation is equipped with the tools and training to help you reach your goals.  In case you needed any other reasons to inquire about what a career with Insurance Nation can do for you check out what else we offer below:

Get paid while you train.  That's right, you will still be earning money while you're learning the ropes.

We will help you get licensed as an insurance professional, and once you're there you will be eligible to start earning commission!
